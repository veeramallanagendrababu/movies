Playback tip

Mobile browsers sometimes block Drive iframe playback. If video does not start, try opening it in a new tab or downloading it directly.

Playback unavailable

If you see "download only": The file's sharing permissions are restricted.
Fix: Right-click the file in Google Drive → Share → Change from "Download only" to "Viewer"

Video Quality Issues?

Why is quality limited even for large files? Google Drive's embedded preview automatically downscales videos to reduce bandwidth.

Choose the right option for your needs:

  • Try High Quality (Google Drive): Opens the file in Google Drive's native viewer - may provide adaptive quality options.
  • Open Preview in New Tab: Uses the embedded player in a full tab - slightly better than the popup embed.
  • Download Video (Full Quality): Best quality - downloads the complete file locally for playback with any media player.
  • For best experience: Download the video if you have storage space - you'll get full resolution and can watch offline.
